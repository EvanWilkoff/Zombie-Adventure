The Zombie Dilemma

Choose your way to survive in this new age apocalypse!

You are watching "Adventure Time" and the news interrupts it with a report of your biggest nightmare. There was a new virus called "Reuben" thats caused by the consumption of expired twinkies. People that consume it turn into man-eating monsters. You just so happened not to go to school because you faked being sick. There is one problem though, your 5 year old brother is stuck in school which is a block away from the first reported zombie incident was at.

Option 1: Go get your brother from school alone while your parents are at work.

Or

Option 2: Stay home and let your parents deal with it.
